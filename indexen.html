Compact Discs

Music CDs, or Compact Discs, revolutionized the music industry with their impeccable audio quality and the ability to store up to 80 minutes of music. These discs follow a standard format with individual tracks, metadata, and a pregap audio track. Despite losing popularity due to digital media, music CDs continue to be a choice for audiophiles and music collectors. They offer a high-fidelity listening experience and are appreciated for their durability.
